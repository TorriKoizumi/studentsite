about

We're Starry Willow, a group of friends who are all passionate about the next generation of thinkers.

Creating jewelry is an outlet, one that helps us to shed away the daily stresses of school. We found that hours of reading articles and writing papers sure puts a lot of strain on the mind. However, bending wire and working with clay helps to alleviate that stress and balances us out!

We feed into our inner arts and crafts artistry and handcraft whimsical jewelry for fashionable professionals. We design our earrings and other jewelry based on what we would want to wear. We take inspiration from our surroundings and interests and then raid Ben Franklin to make it a reality.
