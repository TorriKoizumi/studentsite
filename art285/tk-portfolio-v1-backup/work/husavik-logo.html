Húsavík Logo

Húsavík is a small fishing town located in the northern part of Iceland. It is known for its incredible ocean views, wooden churches, and amazing whale watching tours. It has gained much attention after being the main setting of the Netflix movie Eurovision. With all this new publicity, they will almost certainly be visited by thousands of tourists. Hence the need for a Tourism Logo

Design Rationale

I wanted to focus very heavily on the whale watching tours. Húsavík has a couple noteworty attractions, but nothing quite compares to the up close and personal experience you can have with these beasts.

Client: Húsavík Tourism Agency
Service: Identity
Skills: UX, UI
